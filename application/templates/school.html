Frequently Asked Question

Why should I take exams?

Exams are imperative and plays crucial role in overall development of individual. It plays an important role in the process of learning by assessing the knowledge of students and at the same time exposing their strengths and weaknesses. Therefore, always take exams to evaluate yourself.

Why FirstEdu exams?

FirstEdu exams are spread over wide range of subjects and topics. It tries to cover the areas which are trending in all competitive papers thereby preparing the students to tackle any exams in the world.

Who can take the exams

Any individual can take exams based on his/her requirement. Students can take school based practice papers or skill based competitive exams to sharpen their skills and become masters of their subjects.

What are FirstEdu Olympiads

FirstEdu Olympiads are specially designed exams to find young geniuses across the world. These exams inculcates logical thinking ability, scientific temperament and application of concepts in wider sense.

What are FirstEdu events

FirstEdu events are designed for improving skills of students in extra- curricular activities like writing, speaking, spelling, drawing etc. which are equally important for overall development of an individual.
